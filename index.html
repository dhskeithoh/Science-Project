About our project

We are teaching other people how to survive stranded on an island alone.
This is what you can do if you want to find water that is drinkable.

  1. Find a red cabbage. (A red cabbage is a natural pH indicator and can be used to check the pH of the water)
  2. Dice up a portion of the red cabbage. (This increases surface area to volume ratio)
  3. Fil a pot with water and boil it.
  4. After the water is boiling, pour the diced red cabbage in and boil it.
